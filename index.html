HOW DOES IT WORK

Revolutionize Your Voting Process

Effortless, Secure, and Transparent Online Elections

Conducting online elections is as simple as a 3-step process. With our user-friendly platform, you can seamlessly set up, conduct, and generate results for your elections, ensuring a smooth and efficient voting experience for all.

Step 1

Set-up Election

Setting up your election on eVote is a breeze. In just a few simple steps, you can have your election ready to go

  • Add Election Details
  • Add Voter List
  • Review & Launch



Step 2

Vote

eVote ensures a seamless voting experience for your participants. With a single click, broadcast SMS and emails are sent to notify your voters about the election. They can conveniently record their votes through any internet-enabled device, making the process accessible and user-friendly.

Step 3

Results

Once the election concludes, the approved election officer can generate the results. Our automated system securely tabulates the votes for accuracy and transparency. This additional security measure ensures the integrity of the outcome.
