Features

Dedicated Voting Website

Get a dedicated voting website free for a lifetime with the evote domain for your organization. Eg: http//:xyz.evote.co.in

Compatible with any device

It works seamlessly on all web browsers and internet-enabled devices like laptops, mobile phones, iPads, tablets etc

Customization

Customize your election website with your organization's logo and also choose from available default themes.

Increased voter turnout

SMS and E-mails are sent to your voters to notify them about the election. Reminders to vote are also sent.

Automated system

Since there are no error-prone manual processes, your voters can rest assured that their votes will be counted accurately and instantly.

Candidate information

The system allows you to add candidate information like their profile, photo and designation to the voting website.

Mobile application

We provide an intuitive and robust app with optimal viewing for Android and iOS users.

Voters’ privacy & integrity

Security and confidentiality of the voter are taken very seriously. Voters can only vote once and their voting choices remain anonymous.

Resolute Support Staff

We have a dedicated support team, who can assist you with set-up and help you with quires, if any.

Share and publish a result

Publish and share the outcome of the election with your voters on your voting website. Election results are tabulated electronically in real-time.


Economical
